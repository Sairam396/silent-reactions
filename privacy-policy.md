# Silent Reactions Privacy Policy

Silent Reactions uses the device camera only for real-time hand gesture recognition.

No images, videos, or personal data are stored, shared, or uploaded.
All processing happens entirely on the user's device.

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.
